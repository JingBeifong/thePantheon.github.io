ABOUT THE PANTHEON

THE PANTHEON IS A PERSONAL PROJECT WEBSITE. ITS PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVE IS TO EXPLORE AND EXPERIMENT ON A GREY, MINIMALIST STYLE FOR A WEBSITE. THE PANTHEON'S MAIN THEME IS BASED ON EUROPEAN MARBLE SCULPTURES THAT ARE MOSTLY DERIVED FROM ROMAN AND GREEK MYTHOLOGY. ITS DISTINCT WHITE COLOR AND GREY COLOR IS THIS WEBSITE'S COLOR PALETTE. THIS IS NOT A PROFESSIONAL WEBSITE.
